About Me
I have worked in the fitness industry for 5+ years and have been personally trainer certified for 3 years. Since I was young, fitness has been an important part of my life. I competed in powerlifting competitions for over 7 years, and played various sports growing up including freestyle skiing, soccer, basketball, lacrosse, gymnastics, and competitive track/field. After graduating from Tufts University a year ago with a degree in biology and a focus on exercise science, I opened my conditioning center. My life's passion is fitness and I want to share the joy of healthy living with others.
